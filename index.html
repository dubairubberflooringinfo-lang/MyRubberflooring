What Is Rubber Flooring?

Rubber flooring is a high-performance, slip-resistant, shock-absorbing surface made from natural rubber, recycled rubber, or synthetic rubber compounds. It is widely used in gyms, outdoor spaces, playgrounds, sports courts, villas, warehouses, commercial kitchens, hotels, and industrial facilities.
